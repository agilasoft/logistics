# Welcome to CargoNext

CargoNext is a comprehensive, integrated logistics management platform designed specifically for freight forwarders, customs brokers, truckers, warehouse operators, project cargo specialists, and supply chain managers. Built on the robust Frappe framework, CargoNext brings all your logistics operations under one powerful, easy-to-use system.

## What is CargoNext?

CargoNext is the complete logistics management solution that eliminates the need for multiple disconnected systems. Whether you're managing sea freight, air freight, transportation, customs clearance, warehousing, project cargo, sustainability tracking, or last-mile delivery, CargoNext provides a unified platform to streamline your entire logistics operation.

## Core Modules

### Sea Freight Management

CargoNext's Sea Freight module provides comprehensive ocean freight forwarding capabilities:

- **Complete Shipment Management** - Manage the entire sea freight lifecycle from booking to delivery
- **Container Management** - Track containers, manage container types, and monitor container movements
- **Vessel Schedule Integration** - Access real-time vessel schedules and optimize booking decisions
- **Document Management** - Handle bills of lading, shipping instructions, and all sea freight documentation
- **Rate Management** - Configure and manage sea freight rates, surcharges, and additional charges
- **Port Management** - Track port-to-port movements and manage port operations
- **Customs Integration** - Seamless integration with customs declarations for sea freight shipments
- **Multi-Modal Support** - Connect sea freight with inland transport and warehousing operations

### Air Freight Management

The Air Freight module offers complete air cargo management capabilities:

- **IATA Compliance** - Full compliance with IATA regulations and standards
- **Flight Schedule Integration** - Real-time flight schedule access for better planning and booking
- **Air Waybill Management** - Complete air waybill (AWB) creation, tracking, and management
- **Cargo Booking** - Streamlined booking process with airline integration
- **Rate Calculation** - Automated air freight rate calculation with chargeable weight determination
- **Documentation** - Manage all air freight documentation including commercial invoices, packing lists, and certificates
- **Customs Integration** - Direct integration with customs for air freight clearance
- **Express Shipping Support** - Specialized workflows for express and time-sensitive shipments

### Transport Management

End-to-end transportation management for all your land-based logistics:

- **Complete Transport Job Lifecycle** - Manage transport jobs from pickup to delivery
- **Vehicle Tracking** - Real-time vehicle and shipment tracking capabilities
- **Route Optimization** - Optimize routes for efficiency and cost reduction
- **Fleet Management** - Manage your fleet, drivers, and vehicle assignments
- **Customer Portal** - Provide customers with real-time shipment visibility and tracking
- **Carbon Footprint Calculation** - Track and calculate carbon emissions for transport operations
- **Multi-Stop Management** - Handle complex multi-stop delivery routes
- **Proof of Delivery** - Digital proof of delivery with signature capture and photo documentation
- **Last Mile Integration** - Seamless connection with last-mile delivery operations

### Customs Management

Comprehensive customs brokerage and clearance management:

- **Customs Declaration Management** - Complete customs declaration creation and submission
- **HS Code Management** - Comprehensive Harmonized System (HS) code database and classification
- **Duty and Tax Calculation** - Automated calculation of customs duties, taxes, and fees
- **Customs Broker Assignment** - Assign and track customs brokers for each shipment
- **Clearance Tracking** - Monitor customs clearance status in real-time
- **Document Management** - Manage all customs-related documents including import/export licenses
- **Commodity Classification** - Advanced commodity classification and description management
- **Regulatory Compliance** - Ensure compliance with customs regulations and requirements
- **Multi-Country Support** - Handle customs operations across multiple countries and jurisdictions

### Warehousing Management

Complete warehouse operations management:

- **Warehouse Job Management** - Streamline warehouse job creation and tracking from receipt to dispatch
- **Storage Location Management** - Flexible storage location configuration and hierarchy management
- **Handling Unit Tracking** - Real-time tracking of handling units (pallets, boxes, containers, etc.)
- **Inventory Control** - Comprehensive inventory management with real-time visibility
- **Automated Periodic Billing** - Automated billing for storage charges and value-added services
- **Capacity Management** - Real-time capacity monitoring with alerts and optimization recommendations
- **Value-Added Services (VAS)** - Manage and bill for value-added services like labeling, repacking, and kitting
- **Multi-Warehouse Support** - Manage multiple warehouses and distribution centers from one platform
- **Integration Ready** - Seamless integration with freight and transport operations

### Project Cargo Management

Specialized management for complex, multi-phase project shipments:

- **Multi-Phase Project Tracking** - Manage complex projects with multiple phases and milestones
- **Heavy Lift Management** - Specialized workflows for oversized and heavy cargo
- **Equipment Tracking** - Track specialized equipment and machinery shipments
- **Project Timeline Management** - Visualize and manage project timelines and critical paths
- **Resource Coordination** - Coordinate multiple resources including transport, warehousing, and customs
- **Documentation Management** - Comprehensive documentation for project-specific requirements
- **Cost Management** - Track project costs across all phases and operations
- **Stakeholder Communication** - Enhanced communication tools for project stakeholders

### Sustainability Management

Comprehensive sustainability tracking and environmental impact management:

- **Sustainability Dashboard** - Centralized dashboard for all sustainability metrics
- **Carbon Footprint Calculation** - Calculate carbon emissions across all logistics operations
- **Energy Consumption Tracking** - Monitor energy consumption in warehouses and operations
- **Emission Factor Management** - Configure and manage emission factors for vehicles and operations
- **Sustainability Goals** - Set and track sustainability goals and targets
- **Environmental Impact Reporting** - Comprehensive reporting on environmental impact
- **Green Certification Management** - Track and manage green certifications and compliance
- **Multi-Operation Tracking** - Track sustainability metrics across transport, warehouse, and freight operations

### Last Mile Delivery

Specialized last-mile delivery management:

- **Delivery Route Optimization** - Optimize last-mile delivery routes for efficiency
- **Real-Time Delivery Tracking** - Provide customers with real-time delivery status updates
- **Delivery Window Management** - Manage delivery time windows and customer preferences
- **Proof of Delivery** - Digital proof of delivery with multiple verification methods
- **Customer Communication** - Automated notifications and updates for delivery status
- **Failed Delivery Management** - Handle failed deliveries with rescheduling and exception management
- **Delivery Performance Analytics** - Track and analyze delivery performance metrics
- **Integration with Transport** - Seamless integration with broader transport management

## Key Features

### Integrated Operations
All modules work together seamlessly, eliminating data silos and providing a unified view of your entire logistics operation. Information flows automatically between sea freight, air freight, transport, customs, warehousing, and other modules.

### Real-Time Visibility
Get real-time visibility into all your operations. Track shipments, vehicles, warehouse inventory, and customs clearance status from a single dashboard. Keep your customers informed with live updates.

### Automated Billing
Comprehensive billing system with support for multiple charge types, automated invoice generation, and flexible pricing models. Reduce manual errors and save time with automated billing processes.

### Scalable Architecture
Enterprise-grade architecture that adapts to your unique business model and grows with you. Whether you're a small operation or a large enterprise, CargoNext scales to meet your needs.

### Customer Self-Service Portal
Empower your customers with a portal to track shipments, view documents, and manage orders. Reduce support workload while improving customer satisfaction.

### Advanced Reporting and Analytics
Comprehensive reporting and analytics across all modules. Generate custom reports, track KPIs, and gain insights into your operations for better decision-making.

### Mobile Accessibility
Access CargoNext from any device. Mobile-responsive design ensures you can manage your logistics operations from anywhere, at any time.
