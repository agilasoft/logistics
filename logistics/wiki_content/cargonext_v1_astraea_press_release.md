    # CargoNext v1 Astraea: A New Chapter for Philippine Logistics

    **For immediate release**

    ---

    **Manila, Philippines** — Two Filipino technology companies—**Agilasoft Cloud Technologies Inc.** and **BlueCore Solutions Corp.**—have launched **CargoNext v1 Astraea**, an integrated logistics platform built to serve freight forwarders, customs brokers, truck operators, warehouse operators, and anyone passionate about moving goods smarter.

    Astraea brings together air and sea freight workflows, transport and warehousing jobs, pricing and quoting, billing across modules, and customs declaration handling in one coherent system. Teams can coordinate bookings and shipments, manage charges and profitability, and keep finance and operations aligned—reducing manual handoffs and giving operators a clearer picture from quote to delivery.

    For **freight forwarders and brokers**, the platform supports structured job and shipment management with recognition and policy tooling suited to complex revenue and cost flows. **Truck operators** benefit from transport-focused workflows that tie orders and jobs to the broader chain. **Warehouse operators** get dedicated warehousing capabilities alongside the rest of the pipeline, so inventory-related work sits next to forwarding instead of in a silo. **Logistics enthusiasts**—whether analysts or growing teams—gain a single place to learn how modern forwarding software connects pricing, execution, and compliance.

    Early adopters include **All Transport Network Inc.**, **All Systems Logistics Inc.**, **Prime Warehouse Dynamics Corp.**, **Fast Prime Transport Corp.**, and others—organizations that are already putting CargoNext Astraea to work in real operations.

    CargoNext v1 Astraea reflects a shared belief that world-class logistics software can be imagined and built in the Philippines, for companies that run the region’s supply chains every day.

    **About the partners**  
    **Agilasoft Cloud Technologies Inc.** and **BlueCore Solutions Corp.** collaborate on product vision, implementation, and delivery of the CargoNext platform.
