# Pages Overview

**Pages** are custom views for mobile/field workflows: scanning, job cards, count sheets.

## Transport

- **Run Sheet Scan** (`run-sheet-scan`) – Driver scans and executes Run Sheet legs. Access: Home > Transport > Scan Run Sheet.

## Warehousing

- **Warehouse Job Card** (`warehouse-job-card`) – Execute Warehouse Jobs on handheld. Access: Home > Warehousing > Job Card.
- **Count Sheet** (`count-sheet`) – Physical count entry for stocktake. Access: Home > Warehousing > Count Sheet.
- **Plate Scanner** (`plate_scanner`) – Scan vehicle plates at gate. Access: Home > Warehousing > Plate Scanner.
