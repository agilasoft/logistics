# Air Consolidation

**Air Consolidation** groups multiple air shipments for consolidated carriage. It produces a Master Air Waybill and consolidates cargo from multiple Air Bookings/Shipments.

Industry practice: Consolidators combine shipments to achieve better rates and fill ULDs.

To access: **Home > Air Freight > Air Consolidation**

## 1. Prerequisites

- [Air Booking](welcome/air-booking) or [Air Shipment](welcome/air-shipment) with House Type = Consolidation
- [Air Freight Settings](welcome/air-freight-settings)

## 2. How to Create

1. Go to Air Consolidation list, click **New**.
2. Select **Flight**, **Flight Date**, **ETD**, **ETA**.
3. Add **Packages** from linked Air Shipments.
4. Enter **Master AWB** number.
5. **Save** and **Submit**.
