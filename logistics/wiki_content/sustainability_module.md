# Sustainability Module

**Sustainability** tracks environmental metrics across logistics operations. It integrates with Transport Job, Warehouse Job, Air Shipment, Sea Shipment, Declaration, and General Job to capture carbon footprint, energy consumption, and compliance.

To access: **Home > Sustainability**

## 1. Key Concepts

### 1.1 Sustainability Metrics

**Sustainability Metrics** – Core metrics for tracking emissions, energy, and environmental impact.

### 1.2 Carbon Footprint

**Carbon Footprint** – Tracks carbon emissions from transport and warehouse operations.

### 1.3 Energy Consumption

**Energy Consumption** – Tracks energy usage (electricity, fuel) per facility or operation.

### 1.4 Emission Factors

**Emission Factors** – Conversion factors for calculating emissions from activity data.

### 1.5 Sustainability Goals

**Sustainability Goals** – Targets and KPIs for reduction (e.g., carbon reduction, energy efficiency).

### 1.6 Sustainability Compliance

**Sustainability Compliance** – Compliance status and reporting.

## 2. Module Integration

Sustainability data is linked to:

- Transport Job
- Warehouse Job
- Air Shipment
- Sea Shipment
- Declaration
- General Job

## 3. Reports

- **Sustainability Metrics Report** – Core metrics
- **Sustainability Dashboard Report** – Overview
- **Carbon Footprint Report** – Emissions by source
- **Energy Consumption Report** – Energy usage
- **Sustainability Goals Report** – Goals and progress
- **Sustainability Compliance Report** – Compliance status
- **Cross Module Summary Report** – Cross-module summary
- **Trend Analysis Report** – Trends over time
