# Sea Freight Module

**Sea Freight** (ocean freight) is the transportation of cargo by ship. In CargoNext, the Sea Freight module manages the full lifecycle: Sales Quote → Sea Booking → Sea Shipment → Sea Consolidation (LCL) → Master Bill → Billing.

Industry terms: **FCL** (Full Container Load), **LCL** (Less than Container Load), **TEU**, **Bill of Lading (BL)**, **Incoterms**, **Demurrage**, **Detention**.

To access: **Home > Sea Freight**

## 1. Typical Workflow

1. **Quote** – [Sales Quote](welcome/sales-quote)
2. **Booking** – [Sea Booking](welcome/sea-booking)
3. **Shipment** – [Sea Shipment](welcome/sea-shipment)
4. **Consolidation** (LCL) – [Sea Freight Consolidation](welcome/sea-freight-consolidation)
5. **Master Bill** – [Master Bill](welcome/master-bill)
6. **Billing** – Sales Invoice

## 2. Key Concepts

**FCL vs LCL:** FCL = full container(s); LCL = consolidated cargo from multiple customers.

**House vs Master BL:** House BL for B2B customer; Master BL from carrier to consolidator.
