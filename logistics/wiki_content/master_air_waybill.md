# Master Air Waybill

**Master Air Waybill (MAWB)** is issued by the airline to the freight forwarder for consolidated air cargo. The forwarder issues House AWBs to individual shippers.

Industry practice: One MAWB per consolidated shipment; House AWBs reference it.

To access: **Home > Air Freight > Master Air Waybill**

## 1. Prerequisites

- [Air Consolidation](welcome/air-consolidation)
- Airline, Flight details
