# Reports Overview

**CargoNext** provides comprehensive reports across Sea Freight, Air Freight, Transport, Customs, Warehousing, Job Management, and Sustainability modules. Reports help you analyze performance, billing status, compliance, and operational metrics.

To access reports, go to:

**Home > [Module] > Reports** (from the module workspace)

## 1. Sea Freight Reports

- **Sea Shipment Status Report** – Status of all sea shipments
- **Sea Freight Performance Dashboard** – Key metrics and KPIs
- **Sea Freight Revenue Analysis** – Revenue by route, customer, period
- **Sea Freight Cost Analysis** – Cost breakdown and margins
- **Sea Freight Billing Status Report** – Billed vs unbilled shipments
- **Sea Consolidation Report** – Consolidation utilization
- **Shipping Line Performance Report** – Performance by shipping line
- **Sea Freight Route Analysis** – Volume and performance by route
- **Container Utilization Report** – Container usage
- **On Time Performance Report** – On-time delivery metrics
