# Sea Consolidation

**Sea Consolidation** groups LCL shipments into one container. Produces Master Bill. Access: **Home > Sea Freight > Sea Consolidation**.

## Prerequisites

- [Sea Booking](welcome/sea-booking) or [Sea Shipment](welcome/sea-shipment) with House Type = Consolidation
