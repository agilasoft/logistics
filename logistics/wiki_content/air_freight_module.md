# Air Freight Module

**Air Freight** is the transportation of cargo by aircraft. In CargoNext, the Air Freight module manages: Sales Quote → Air Booking → Air Shipment → Air Consolidation → Master Air Waybill → Billing.

Industry terms: **AWB** (Air Waybill), **ULD** (Unit Load Device), **Chargeable Weight**, **IATA**, **Dangerous Goods (DG)**.

To access: **Home > Air Freight**

## 1. Typical Workflow

1. **Quote** – [Sales Quote](welcome/sales-quote)
2. **Booking** – [Air Booking](welcome/air-booking)
3. **Shipment** – [Air Shipment](welcome/air-shipment)
4. **Consolidation** – [Air Consolidation](welcome/air-consolidation)
5. **Master AWB** – [Master Air Waybill](welcome/master-air-waybill)
6. **Billing** – Sales Invoice

## 2. Key Concepts

**Chargeable Weight:** max(gross weight, volumetric weight). Volumetric = volume(cm³)/6000 (IATA).

**ULD:** Pallets, containers for aircraft. Define in [ULD Type](welcome/uld-type).

## 3. Workspace Structure

**Quick Access:** Sales Quote, Air Booking, Air Shipment, Air Consolidation, Master Air Waybill, Sales Invoice

**Masters:** [Airline](welcome/airline), [ULD Type](welcome/uld-type)

**Reports:** Air Shipment Status Report, Air Consolidation Report, On-Time Performance Report, Dangerous Goods Compliance Report, Air Freight Revenue/Cost Analysis, Billing Status Report, Air Freight Performance Dashboard, Airline Performance Report
